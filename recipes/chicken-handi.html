Chicken Handi

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a handi or deep pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  4. Add the pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates from the masala.
  5. Add the yogurt and mix well. Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Add garam masala and mix well. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
