Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil or parsley

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and fresh herbs. Simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with a beaten egg and a pinch of salt.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles, then a layer of the ricotta mixture, and a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
