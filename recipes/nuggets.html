Nuggets

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp minced onion
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, milk, minced onion, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly distributed.
  2. Shape the mixture into 12 equal portions and roll each into a ball. Flatten each ball into a patty about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. In a large skillet or deep fryer, heat the vegetable oil to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Carefully place the nuggets in the hot oil and fry for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove the nuggets from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
